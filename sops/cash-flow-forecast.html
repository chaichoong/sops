🎯 1. Purpose & Scope

Generates a combined 30-day rolling cash flow forecast for the Santander and TNT Mgt Zempler accounts using live Airtable data. Pulls synced opening balances, projects rental income from active tenancies, and maps fixed costs due within the window. Outputs an interactive HTML dashboard with a chart, daily breakdown, and risk flags.

ℹ️
Scope: In scope: 30-day forward forecast, money in (tenancies) and money out (costs), risk flagging. Out of scope: historical analysis, individual account forecasts, or any Airtable writes — this skill is read-only.

💡 2. When to Use This Skill

💬 Trigger Phrases

Use this skill when Kevin or the team says any of the following:

  • "Forecast cash flow" / "What does cash flow look like?"
  • "Check what's coming in and out"
  • "Plan payments for the next 30 days"
  • "Predict upcoming balances"
⚠️
Do not use this skill when: Analysing historical transactions — use the transaction reconciler for past data.

1

Get Opening Balance

automated
  1. Fetch Santander (rec3LiEiifomEHlvy) and TNT Mgt Zempler (recsR9QhRKYwgV8oP) from Accounts table.
  2. Read the **GBP field (fldhDG5jDA8Tu2JyI) from each — this is the live synced balance.
  3. Sum both to get the combined opening balance. Note today as Day 0.
2

Load Active Costs and Tenancies

automated
  1. Costs: fetch all from Costs table, filter by Payment Status (In Payment, Active, Overdue, Due Today, Upcoming) and Inactive = false.
  2. Tenancies: fetch all, filter by Payment Status (Unified) = In Payment or CFV Actioned.
  3. CRITICAL: Do NOT use Paid This Period? or Paid This Month? fields for any filtering.
ℹ️
Due Day of Month is the sole determinant for when a payment falls due. If due day ≥ today → this month. If due day < today → next month.
3

Project Due Dates onto 30-Day Grid

automated
  1. For each day 0–30: calculate Money In (rent due) and Money Out (costs due).
  2. Monthly items: apply Due Day of Month logic. Weekly/fortnightly/4-weekly: anchor from Due Date (Next), project at interval.
  3. Ending Balance = Previous Day Ending Balance + Money In − Money Out.
4

Output HTML Dashboard

automated
  1. Generate HTML file: cash-flow-forecast-YYYY-MM-DD.html with dark theme styling.
  2. Include: KPI cards row (6 cards), Chart.js line chart, interactive daily breakdown table (clickable rows), risk flags section, footer.
  3. Risk flags: negative balance, balance < £500, single cost > £1,000, concentration of large costs on same day.
  4. Provide brief chat summary: opening balance, net change, final balance, lowest balance date, key risks.

🚨 8. Error Handling

  • Airtable API error on fetch: Report clearly and continue with available data.
  • Stale balance warning: Check Last Successful Update field. If stale, warn Kevin before showing forecast.
  • Cost has no Due Day of Month: Skip it and note in a footnote.
  • Tenancy has no Expected Monthly Rent: Skip it and note in a footnote.

🛠️ 9. Troubleshooting

❌ Forecast shows payments already made this month
✅ Do NOT use Paid This Period? fields. Only the Due Day of Month logic should determine inclusion. Past due days map to next month automatically.
❌ Non-monthly costs not appearing correctly
✅ Use Due Date (Next) field as anchor for weekly/fortnightly/4-weekly costs and project forward at correct interval.
